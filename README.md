# Career Ladder

## Software Engineer (IC1)

|Competence| Description                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       |
|---|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
|Feedback| <li>Asks and seeks feedback from their squadmates, and managers in a useful way. <br/><li>Expected to receive feedback from their manager, peers, and team members on a regular basis.                                                                                                                                                |
|Communication| <li>Communicates effectively in written and verbal form technical subjects, to their teammates.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       |
|Teamwork| <li>Openly shares their opinions, knowledge and contributes to discussions in a respectful manner. <br/><li>Works with teammates to resolve disagreements in a healthy manner. <br/><li>Is open to changing their perspective and plans based on others' input.                                                                               |
|Project organization| <li>Acts according to task prioritization. <br/><li>Has daily conversations with the team about the progress of their work. <br/><li>Delivers commitments with a sense of urgency.                                                                                                                                                            |
|Domain knowledge| <li>High-level understanding of the team domain and a good understanding of the services they work on. <br/><li>They may also have a basic understanding of the company's vision and mission and some crypto industry knowledge.                                                                                                          |
|Writing code| <li>Can write simple, clean and testable code that meets basic requirements, but may require some guidance on code quality. <br/><li>Good understanding of basic programming concepts (like OOO, functional...). <br/><li>Actively participates in code reviews, learns from feedback provided by peers, and incorporates it into their work. |
|Software design| <li>Understands the overall  service architecture and is able to follow established architecture/design patterns.                                                                                                                                                                                                                     |
|Troubleshooting| <li>Troubleshoot basic issues related to their own code, and have a basic understanding of observability and monitoring tools and metrics used in their team. <br/><li>Escalates more complex issues to the senior colleagues.                                                                                                            |
|Decision making| <li>Understands the goals, plans and roadmaps for the team. <br/><li>Holds themselves accountable for decisions and outcomes.                                                                                                                                                                                                             |
|Mentoring| <li>Primarily focused on their own learning and growth, seeking guidance from more experienced colleagues. <br/><li>Able and willing to identify areas for mentorship.                                                                                                                                                                    |
|Process thinking| <li>Understands and follows the software development processes and practices set by the team.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
